Element Details
Pitches (i) In this session, you will introduce your visualization sketch to the rest of the group. You will have 2 slides and 2 minutes for your pitch. Slide 1 should only show a project title and an image (e.g., from unsplash.com.) that helps you introduce and motivate your research question. Slide 2 should only show your hand-drawn visualization sketch and a slide title. No bullet points allowed. Be aware that 2 minutes pass by quickly. Please practice and ideally time your presentation beforehand.

(ii) After each presentation there will be 3 minutes for peer feedback, so please be prepared to comment on fellow participants’ presentations. We will provide our feedback individually via Teams after the session.

(iii) To ensure a swift transition between presentations submit your pitch presentation slides until Thursday, September 28th, 2023, 11:59pm via this Dropbox Upload - Naming convention: 'Pitch_LASTNAME.ppt'.
Project work (i) In the project work session you will have time to ask questions to me and your peers, discuss ideas and prepare your analysis / presentation.
Presentation (i) In this session, you will communicate the results of your research question using your final visualization. As for the pitch, you will have 2 slides—an image-based intro slide and a slide for your final visualization—but you will this time have 3 minutes for your presentation. Again, no bullet points or text besides titles. Practice and time your presentation beforehand. This is your moment to shine–tell a good story using your compelling data visualization!

(ii) To ensure a swift transition between presentations, we ask you to submit your final presentation slides until Thursday, December 7th, 2023, 11:59pm via this Dropbox upload - Naming convention: 'Presentation_LASTNAME.ppt'.
Final report The final assignment of this course is to compile and submit a one-to-two-page report. The report should include a (i) catchy title and abstract (max 50 words), (ii) text presenting the research question, the first scatch, the methodology and data, the results and conclusions, and, of course, (iii) the final data visualization. The report should be created using R quarto and submitted until December 20th, 2023, 11:59pm as a pdf or single html file via this Dropbox upload - Naming convention: 'FinalReport_LASTNAME.[pdf|html]'.
